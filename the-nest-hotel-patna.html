About Us

A Home Away
From Home

The Nest Hotel is one of Patna's most beloved budget luxury properties, nestled in the heart of Kankarbagh, conveniently located near Medanta Hospital on P.C. Colony Road. We have been providing warm, comfortable, and affordable stays to travelers, business professionals, and families alike.

Our thoughtfully designed rooms, attentive staff, and modern amenities ensure every guest feels at home. Whether you're in Patna for work or leisure, The Nest is your perfect base — centrally located with easy access to Patna Junction, the airport, and all major landmarks.
